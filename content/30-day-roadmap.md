# 30-Day FastStart Roadmap
## Your Week-by-Week Guide to Recruiting Your First Contractors

---

## Week 1: Foundation & First Outreach

### Day 1-2: Setup & Preparation
**Tasks:**
- [ ] Review the First Conversation Script (practice it 3 times)
- [ ] Set up your Contractor Tracker Spreadsheet
- [ ] Make a list of 10 contractors you know or want to reach out to
- [ ] Choose 3 social media templates to post this week
- [ ] Set your goal: How many contractors will you recruit this month? (Recommended: 3-5)

**Time:** 2-3 hours

---

### Day 3-4: First Outreach
**Tasks:**
- [ ] Send your first recruitment email (use Email #1 from swipe file)
- [ ] Make your first social media post
- [ ] Reach out to 2 contractors via text/DM using the conversation script

**Goal:** Start 3 conversations with potential contractors

**Time:** 1-2 hours

---

### Day 5-7: Follow-Up & Momentum
**Tasks:**
- [ ] Follow up with anyone who didn't respond to your first outreach
- [ ] Post your second social media template
- [ ] Have your first conversation with a contractor who responded
- [ ] Update your Contractor Tracker with all activity

**Goal:** Have at least 1 conversation with a potential contractor

**Time:** 1-2 hours

**Week 1 Success Metrics:**
- 3+ outreach attempts made
- 1+ conversations had
- 2+ social media posts published

---

## Week 2: Conversations & Conversions

### Day 8-10: Conversation Focus
**Tasks:**
- [ ] Reach out to 3 more contractors
- [ ] Schedule conversations with anyone who responded last week
- [ ] Send follow-up emails to contractors you spoke with
- [ ] Post your third social media template

**Goal:** Have 2-3 conversations this week

**Time:** 2-3 hours

---

### Day 11-12: Objection Handling
**Tasks:**
- [ ] Review objection handlers in the First Conversation Script
- [ ] Follow up with contractors who said "let me think about it"
- [ ] Send Email #3 (Problem Agitation) to your list

**Goal:** Move 1 contractor from "interested" to "signed up"

**Time:** 1-2 hours

---

### Day 13-14: Momentum Building
**Tasks:**
- [ ] Celebrate your first win (even if it's just a conversation)
- [ ] Post another social media template
- [ ] Reach out to 2 more contractors
- [ ] Review what's working and what's not

**Goal:** 1 contractor signed up OR 3+ active conversations in progress

**Time:** 1-2 hours

**Week 2 Success Metrics:**
- 2+ conversations had
- 1+ contractor signed up OR 3+ in active follow-up
- 4+ total social media posts published

---

## Week 3: Scaling & Systems

### Day 15-17: Double Down
**Tasks:**
- [ ] Analyze which outreach method is working best (email, social, direct)
- [ ] Do more of what's working
- [ ] Reach out to 5 new contractors
- [ ] Post 2 social media templates

**Goal:** Have 3-4 active conversations happening

**Time:** 2-3 hours

---

### Day 18-19: Follow-Up Blitz
**Tasks:**
- [ ] Follow up with EVERY contractor you've talked to
- [ ] Send Email #7 (Time objection handler) to your list
- [ ] Check in with contractors who signed up — how are they doing?

**Goal:** Convert 1-2 more contractors from "interested" to "signed up"

**Time:** 1-2 hours

---

### Day 20-21: Review & Adjust
**Tasks:**
- [ ] Review your Contractor Tracker — where is everyone in the pipeline?
- [ ] Identify bottlenecks (are people not responding? not converting?)
- [ ] Adjust your approach based on what's working
- [ ] Post another social media template

**Goal:** 2+ contractors signed up by end of Week 3

**Time:** 1-2 hours

**Week 3 Success Metrics:**
- 2+ contractors signed up
- 5+ conversations had
- 6+ social media posts published
- Clear sense of what's working

---

## Week 4: Momentum & Future Planning

### Day 22-24: Push for Your Goal
**Tasks:**
- [ ] Reach out to 5 more contractors
- [ ] Heavy follow-up with anyone on the fence
- [ ] Post 2 social media templates
- [ ] Ask for referrals from contractors who signed up

**Goal:** Hit your monthly contractor goal (3-5 contractors)

**Time:** 2-3 hours

---

### Day 25-26: Referral Requests
**Tasks:**
- [ ] Contact every contractor who signed up and ask: "Do you know any other contractors who might want leads?"
- [ ] Send Email #9 (Social Media Angle) to your list
- [ ] Post about your success (without revealing numbers)

**Sample post:** "Just added another great contractor to my referral network. If you're a [trade] in [area] and want consistent leads, let's talk."

**Goal:** Get 1-2 contractor referrals from your existing network

**Time:** 1-2 hours

---

### Day 27-28: Month-End Review
**Tasks:**
- [ ] Count your total contractors signed up
- [ ] Calculate your monthly recurring income (contractors × $20-40)
- [ ] Review your Contractor Tracker — what patterns do you see?
- [ ] Document lessons learned

**Questions to ask yourself:**
- What worked best? (Email, social media, direct outreach?)
- What objections were hardest to overcome?
- What would you do differently?
- What will you focus on next month?

**Time:** 1-2 hours

---

### Day 29-30: Plan Month 2
**Tasks:**
- [ ] Set your Month 2 goal (double your Month 1 goal?)
- [ ] Make a list of 20 new contractors to reach out to
- [ ] Schedule your Month 2 outreach
- [ ] Celebrate your wins!

**Time:** 1-2 hours

**Week 4 Success Metrics:**
- Monthly goal achieved (3-5 contractors)
- Recurring income calculated
- Month 2 planned
- Lessons documented

---

## Monthly Success Checklist

By the end of 30 days, you should have:

- [ ] **3-5 contractors signed up** (or more!)
- [ ] **$60-200/month in recurring income** locked in
- [ ] **A system that works** for your personality and market
- [ ] **Confidence** in your recruiting abilities
- [ ] **Momentum** heading into Month 2

---

## Time Commitment

**Total time per week:** 5-8 hours
**Total time for 30 days:** 20-30 hours

**Breakdown:**
- Outreach: 2-3 hours/week
- Conversations: 1-2 hours/week
- Social media: 1 hour/week
- Follow-up: 1-2 hours/week

**ROI:** If you recruit 5 contractors at $30/month average = $150/month recurring
**Payback period:** 2-3 months to recover your time investment
**Year 1 potential:** $1,800+ in recurring income from 30 hours of work

---

## If You Get Stuck

**Problem:** No one is responding to my outreach
**Solution:** Try a different channel (if email isn't working, try social media or direct text)

**Problem:** Contractors are interested but not signing up
**Solution:** Review the objection handlers. Follow up more. Create urgency.

**Problem:** I don't have time
**Solution:** Start with just 3 hours/week. Even 1 contractor per month adds up.

**Problem:** I'm nervous about the conversations
**Solution:** Practice the script 5 times before your first call. Remember: you're helping, not selling.
