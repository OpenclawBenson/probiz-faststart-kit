## How to Use These Templates

1. **Copy and paste** the template into your social media platform
2. **Customize** [your area] with your actual location
3. **Add** your own personality and voice
4. **Post consistently** (3-5 times per week)
5. **Respond to every comment and DM** within 24 hours
6. **Track results** in your Contractor Tracker Spreadsheet

## Pro Tips

- **Mix up the categories** — don't just post CTAs
- **Use images** when possible (before/after work photos, team photos)
- **Engage with comments** — the algorithm loves conversation
- **Post at optimal times** — typically 9am, 12pm, and 6pm
- **Be consistent** — one post won't change your business, but 20 will

---

**Remember:** These posts position you as a connector and solution provider. You're not begging for business — you're offering valuable connections.
