# ProBiz FastStart Kit - Deliverables

## Overview
The ProBiz FastStart Kit is a complete recruiting system for new ProBiz members who want to build their contractor network and earn $20-40/month in recurring income per contractor.

**Price:** $37  
**Format:** Digital download (PDFs, templates, video)

---

## What's Included

### 1. Email Swipe File (10 Recruitment Emails)
**File:** `content/email-swipe-file.md`

10 copy-and-paste email templates designed to get contractors to respond and book calls.

**Categories:**
- Opportunity Introduction
- Social Proof Angle
- Problem Agitation
- Value Stack
- Urgency/Scarcity
- Story/Origin
- Objection Handler (Time)
- Objection Handler (Experience)
- Social Media Angle
- Direct Ask

**How to use:** Copy, customize with your name/details, paste into Gmail, send.

---

### 2. First Conversation Script
**File:** `content/first-conversation-script.md`

Word-for-word script for recruiting contractors in 15-minute conversations.

**Includes:**
- The 3-question framework
- ProBiz introduction (30 seconds)
- Objection handlers for common responses
- Soft close techniques
- Post-conversation follow-up email
- Pro tips for success

**How to use:** Practice the script 3 times, then use it on real calls.

---

### 3. Social Media Post Templates (20 Posts)
**File:** `content/social-media-templates.md`

20 pre-written social media posts to attract contractors organically.

**Categories:**
- Problem Awareness (5 posts)
- Solution Presentation (5 posts)
- Social Proof (5 posts)
- Direct Call to Action (5 posts)

**Platforms:** Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn

**How to use:** Copy, customize [your area], add your personality, post 3-5x per week.

## Quick Start Guide

### For Customers (People Who Buy the Kit)

1. **Download all files** from the delivery page
2. **Watch the walkthrough video** first (10 minutes)
3. **Read the 30-Day Roadmap** to understand the big picture
4. **Start with Week 1 tasks:**
   - Set up your Contractor Tracker
   - Make a list of 10 contractors to contact
   - Send your first email using Template #1
5. **Follow the roadmap** day by day
6. **Track your progress** and celebrate wins!

### For You (Setting Up the Product)

1. **Review all content** to ensure quality
2. **Record the walkthrough video** (optional but recommended)
3. **Create the Contractor Tracker** Google Sheets template
4. **Package everything** into a clean download
5. **Set up delivery** (email with download link, or member area)
6. **Test the purchase flow** end-to-end
7. **Launch!**
