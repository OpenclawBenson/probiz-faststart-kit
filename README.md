### For ProBiz Business Owners:
1. **Deploy the Sales Page** - Add to your website to sell the kit

## Customization Tips

### Personalizing the Content:
- Replace `[Your Name]` with your actual name
- Replace `[Your City]` with your local area
- Update `[Your Contact Info]` with your details
- Add your own success stories and testimonials
- Adjust pricing mentions if your structure differs

### Making It Your Own:
- Add your voice and personality to emails
- Include local references and examples
- Share your own "why" and story
- Use photos of yourself where appropriate
